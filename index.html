About this project

It is a robotic arm with a brain of its own which can play chess like any human can do with another robot or human.

Features

  • A Brain - Brain is a system for thinking which moves should the robot play and how should the robotic arm execute it.

  • A robotic arm - Robotic arm is the mechanical system which just looks like a human arm and will move the chess pieces on board as per the commands given by the brain.

  • A sensory chess board - Sensory board is a chess board fitted with sensors for getting information of the location of all pieces on the board.
